--- layout: post title: The troubles with searching on Synology NAS volumes date: '2019-11-07T12:54:00.001Z' author: Thomas Tempelmann tags: modified_time: '2019-11-07T12:54:56.783Z' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-8797526579651167122.post-4862519934495363955 blogger_orig_url: https://blog.tempel.org/2019/11/the-troubles-with-searching-on-synology.html --- Iterating over all directories via AFP is much faster than via SMB.

Spotlight (called Universal Search in Synology's Web UI) is not working right:

Via SMB, it may not find any or all results unless you use the "-live" option.
Via AFP, it works without the "-live" option, but it never finishes.
Meaning that in both cases, you cannot tell when the search has finished.
